Brams School Presents

Music Training
For Schools

Music Training For Schools (MTFS) is a music programme designed for young children of all ages (pupils/students) who desire to learn one or two musical instruments.It is a fun group class experience, educative, great for beginners who have little or no background in music.

Brams Music School adopts the standard curriculum of The Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM), which will enable the pupils/students to know some basic rudiments and theory of music. We would be glad to impact on your children and teach them how to read, write and play music.
